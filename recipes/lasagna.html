Lasagna

Worlds Best Lasagna!

Indulge in the world's best lasagna, a culinary masterpiece that tantalizes the taste buds and warms the soul with every bite. This iconic Italian dish boasts layers upon layers of rich and flavorful ingredients meticulously crafted to perfection. Imagine tender sheets of homemade pasta, generously smothered in a robust and savory meat sauce, interspersed with creamy ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheeses. Each layer harmoniously blends with fragrant herbs, garlic, and onions, creating a symphony of flavors that dance on your palate. Baked to golden perfection, the top layer offers a delightful crispy texture that seals in the lusciousness beneath. Whether you're sharing it with loved ones or savoring it all to yourself, this lasagna is guaranteed to be a show-stopping sensation that leaves everyone craving for more.

Ingredients

Meat Sauce

  • 250g Beef Mince
  • 250g Pork Mince
  • 2 Medium Onions
  • 2 Stalks Celery
  • 2 Carrots
  • 3 Cloves Garlic
  • 500g Canned San Marzano Tomatos
  • 1/4 Cup Fresh Parsely
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Dried Italian Herbs
  • 1/4 Cup Milk

Bechamel Sauce

  • 50g Butter
  • 1/4 Cup Plain Flour
  • 2 1/2 Cups Milk

Other

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mozzarells Cheese

Method

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodle.s
  3. Make bechamel sauce.
  4. Layer lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake in the oven 190c for 50 mins.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
